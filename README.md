# NAVBEATS 🎶🧭

**Your route. Your rhythm.**

NAVBEATS is a route-aware music app that generates playlists tailored to your journey.
Enter a starting point and destination, and NAVBEATS analyzes the trip to create a soundtrack that matches the **distance, pace, and overall vibe** of the route.

The goal is to turn everyday navigation into a dynamic listening experience — whether you're driving across the city or walking through a neighborhood.

---

## ✨ Features (in progress)

* Route input for **origin and destination**
* Map visualization with interactive markers
* Automatic geocoding of locations
* Map zooming to fit selected journey
* Playlist generation based on route characteristics *(planned)*

---

## 🧰 Tech Stack

* React
* TypeScript
* Vite
* Leaflet (map rendering)
* OpenStreetMap / Nominatim (geocoding)
* Spotify Web API *(planned)*

## 💡 Concept

NAVBEATS explores the intersection of **navigation and music**.

Instead of choosing music first, the **journey itself becomes the input**.
Your path shapes the soundtrack.

---

## 📌 Inspiration

Driving playlists, walking soundtracks, and the idea that **movement through space can influence how we listen to music.**
